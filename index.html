I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of South Florida. My work focuses on understanding how identity cues (e.g., nationality, gender, race, age) influence model behavior, especially for subjective judgments like emotion attribution, safety/toxicity, and cultural norm interpretation.

I design datasets and evaluation protocols, run large-scale experiments across LLMs and multimodal models, and work on reliable assessment methods that help distinguish true capability from bias or spurious patterns.
