Mahammed Kamruzzaman

I am a PhD Student at the University of South Florida's Department of Computer Science and Engineering. I received my Bachelor of Science at Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology, Bangladesh. My research interests include natural language processing, data mining and machine learning. My undergraduate thesis was in the intersection of machine learning and sentiment analysis. I was also involved with deep learning based research activities for different applications e.g., disease detection and bangla handwritten digit recognition.
